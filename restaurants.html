Discover the Culinary Delights of Taniti

  • Three restaurants bring a taste of home with classic American-style meals. From juicy burgers to hearty breakfasts, comfort food is always on the menu.
  • Two restaurants embrace the rich diversity of Pan-Asian cuisine, offering dishes inspired by the kitchens of Thailand, Japan, and beyond.
