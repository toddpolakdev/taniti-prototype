Entertainment

Exciting Entertainment Options in Taniti

Taniti is best known for its breathtaking beaches, lush rainforests, and the awe-inspiring volcano. But beyond these iconic attractions, there’s a wealth of activities to suit every interest:

  • Immerse yourself in the island’s culture by visiting a local history museum or exploring vibrant art galleries.
  • Dive into adventure with chartered fishing tours, snorkeling, or zip-lining through the rainforest.
  • Unwind with nightlife options, including several pubs, a microbrewery, and a new dance club where you can dance the night away.
  • Enjoy family-friendly activities like visiting an arcade, bowling, or catching the latest film at the movie theater.
  • For a bird’s-eye view of the island, take an unforgettable helicopter ride.
  • A nine-hole golf course is also in development and is expected to open next year, adding even more to the entertainment scene.

Many of these activities are concentrated in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay, making it a hotspot for visitors and locals alike.
